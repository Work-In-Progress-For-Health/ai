RAG status definitation
- Red: Off track, escalation required (<70% of target trajectory)
- Amber: At risk, intervention needed (70-89% of target trajectory)
- Green: On track to achieve target (≥90% of target trajectory)

Escalation process
1. KPI moves to Amber → Owner develops recovery plan within 5 days
2. KPI remains Amber → Review at monthly Leadership Team meeting
3. KPI moves to Red → Immediate escalation to Executive sponsor
4. OKR at risk → Executive Committee review and intervention
